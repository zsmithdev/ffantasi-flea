Goods

As you'd expect from a Flea Market, you can find all sorts at Ffantasi. Vintage clothes, crafty gifts, vinyl records, and antique homewares to name just a few. With different traders for each event, there is something for everyone. More of a visual learner? Check out our gallery to get an idea of the vibe at a Ffantasi Flea event.

Traders

Our traders are the best. We have trusty locals and regulars, providing a friendly face and quality service at Ffantasi. However, we are always open to new traders who like what they see and want to bring their unique goods or services to our events. Interested? Check out our Traders page for more information and to sign-up to trade.
