Traders

Our traders are what make Ffantasi Flea Market. Though every event will look a little bit different, we have some core traders who attend every Ffantasi event. That balance of trusted favourites and innovative newcomers is what defines Ffantasi Flea Markets. We're always looking for new traders, so if you see a gap in the market at our events, sign up to become a trader using the form below.

Roland's Retro Wears

Think 70s dagger collar shirts, 80s colour-block track jackets and 90s baggy denim. Roland brings you curated vintage clothing and accessories with prices that can't be beaten.

Betsy's Bookshop

Whether you're into your political non-fiction or steamy romance novels, Betsy has you covered. With a constantly updated collection of quality second-hand books, you're sure to find a new read or old favourite in her collection.

Vinyl Grooves

The vinyl haul of the century, Vinyl Grooves is a Ffantasi Flea regular for good reason. Records are back in style in a big way and owner Vince will be able to recommend where to start with your selection.

Back to Bric-a-Brac

Ornate silverware. Hand carved picture frames. Colourful vases. Decorative vintage keys. No two markets are the same for Back to Bric-a-Brac, a family run stall specialising in just about anything. Adorning your home with unique pieces has never been easier.

Magpie Mike

Part-time cobbler, full-time metal detector and collector, Mike is a bit of a local legend. Bring your steel toe-capped boots for same day refurbishing, and purchase antique steel goods in the meantime.

Comic Youth

As part of the Cardiff Youth Group, Comic Youth is run by avid comic collectors looking to improve their social skills and become business savvy. Part social action project, part geek haven, with comics ranging from mint condition classics to modern adaptions.

Want to trade at Ffantasi Flea Market?

Fill out our trader sign-up form with your business information and contact details, and we'll let you know if you sound like a fit for our market.
